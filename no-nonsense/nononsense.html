No-Nonsense

A chrome (and Firefox) extension that blocks paid ads in your searches

Version - 1.0

About

No-Nonsense is an extension which removes any search ads from your google searches. It is completely free to use.

This is good for anybody who wants to see what searches are the most popular, rather than what company paid the most money to advertise.




What are search ads?

Search ads are ads for websites that are displayed when you search for something on a web browser. This is done to make the browser a little extra money. However, this can lead to unhelpful websites being displayed first in a search, rather than the most visited. This is bad for many reasons, and is the reason we have made this extension.
